  • No One Wins

    No One Wins is a campaign that informs people about working conditions of nurses within indigenous communities are a no win situation for them and the people in their care. The campaign engages the public to play a kiosk based Breakout type game in which it's impossible to break out from working conditions. By playing the game, they learn what nurses are up against and how they can help. Developed in partnership with Carmen Huang and Liza Borissova.
